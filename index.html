Our Sizzling Story since 2019

Davao's Pride in Every Juicy Bite

Polangco Fried Chicken was founded with a singular, fierce obsession: building a quick-service brand that honors the deep, comforting art of the perfect fried chicken. While others centralized kitchens and cut costs with mechanical processing, we chose the harder route.

We spent over two years testing spices in our kitchen in Tagum, crafting a signature 11-spice marinade that penetrates deep past the bone and designing specialized heavy pressure fryers. When chicken is fried under precise pressure, moisture can’t escape. It yields that legendary Polangco signature: thin, crackling-crisp skin on the outside holding in rushing hot, savory meat juices inside.
